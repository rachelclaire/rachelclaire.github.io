PowerUp Logo

Digital

Fall 2014

Given a description of a hypothetical power bar company, I created a logo that represented the company's product and values. My logo uses a flame to symbolize adventure, energy, and the outdoors, appealing to the targetted group that would purchase this power bar. I also applied the logo to various branding materials.
